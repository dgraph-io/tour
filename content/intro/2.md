Press **run** on the panel to the right. This loads a schema that the first lessons require. Later lessons explain the syntax in detail; for now, confirm in the response panel that the operation succeeded.

{{% notice "note" %}} Schema changes require Alter operations. All clients provide Mutate, Query, and Alter operations. {{% /notice %}}
