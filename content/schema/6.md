The reverse edge of `anEdge` is `~anEdge`.

The query below finds everyone who works for "CompanyABC" by traversing
`works_for` in reverse (`~works_for`) instead of adding a separate edge from
company to person. The alias `works_here` labels the result so the output reads
clearly.
